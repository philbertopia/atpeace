Info

Atpeace.com is a platform that documents and sells physical artworks. Differentiating from a traditional gallery, our website is a way to buy artwork from talented artists while funding vital innovative projects.


10% of each sale goes to atpeace.com for pairing paintings, furniture and sculptures with deserving projects that align with the intention and worldview of the artist. The remaining 90% is split between the artist and the chosen recipient of funding.


Our mission is to explore new ways that making and buying art can produce and support new ideas in need of funding. We believe this system has the potential to generate an exciting cultural landscape through interdisciplinary cross pollination and comradery between artists, collectors and innovators.
